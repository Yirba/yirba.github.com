About the poll

07th Expansion regularly run polls on their website where fans can vote for their favourite characters from a particular game. At the end of the poll, a ranking of the characters is published.

This time, things are a bit different. Firstly, there are four polls this time, one for each of four games. (I'm guessing these games are Higurashi, Umineko, Higanbana and Rose Guns Days.) Secondly, the polls are integrated with Twitter, so be sure to create an account there before you start.

How to vote

First, visit the homepage of the 07th Expansion character poll. There, you will find a link to the latest poll. Click it, and you will be presented with the current ranking. You will then need to sign into Twitter by clicking the appropriate button:
